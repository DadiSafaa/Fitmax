Our Trainer

At FITMAX, we pride ourselves on having a team of highly skilled and dedicated trainers.

Amine El Yousfi

Weightlifting

Amine El Yousfi is a dedicated weightlifting trainer with over a decade of experience in the field. He has guided countless athletes to achieve their personal bests, focusing on proper technique and injury prevention. Amine’s approach to training is holistic, combining strength training with nutritional advice and mental coaching. Follow him on social media for daily workout tips and motivational content.

Sara Benomar

Weightlifting

Sara Benomar is a seasoned weightlifting trainer known for her expertise and inspiring coaching style. With more than 15 years in the industry, Sara has helped athletes of all levels reach their goals, from beginners to competitive lifters. Her training programs are tailored to individual needs, emphasizing strength, endurance, and overall fitness. Connect with Sara on social media to stay updated on her training sessions and fitness advice.

Weightlifting
